About Kingston

Your Neighbourhood's Wine & Beer Haven

Kingston Wines & Beer was born from a passion for great drinks and great conversations. With convenient stores across Andheri East, Seepz, and Vile Parle — we're your go-to neighbourhood destination for quality beverages. Kingston57, our flagship in Vile Parle, opened in August 2024 and has quickly become the area's most loved wine & beer shop.

Expertly Curated Selection

Hand-picked red, white, rosé & sparkling wines alongside domestic and international beers — chosen for quality, taste and value.

Knowledgeable Staff

Our team is always on hand to guide you to the perfect bottle for any occasion, mood, or budget — no question too big or small.
